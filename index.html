Tiramisu Temptation

Tiramisu

This recipe is adapted from "Classic Tiramisu" by Natasha Kravchuk and can be found on natashaskitchen.com.

Ingredients

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 8 oz mascarpone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups brewed espresso (cooled)
  • 2 packs of ladyfinger cookies
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Equipment

  • Mixing bowls
  • Electric mixer
  • Spatula
  • 8x8 glass baking dish
  • Sifter

Directions

  1. In a bowl, whisk egg yolks and sugar until thick and pale. Set over simmering water and whisk until slightly warm.
  2. In a separate bowl, whip the cream until stiff peaks form.
  3. In another bowl, mix mascarpone with vanilla until smooth, then gently fold in whipped cream.
  4. Quickly dip each ladyfinger in espresso and line the bottom of your dish.
  5. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and the rest of the mascarpone mix.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 6 hours. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.
